Breads

White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4T salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettle, larg mixing bowl, large mixing spoon (wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap, dish

Steps to make Bread

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very larg bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup. If you don't have a large measuring cup, combine ingredients in a pot.
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water.When you only have about a half a cup left, feel the water and keep stirring and feeling the mixed liquids as you add the water. If it's too hot, add a little cold water. If it's too cold, add more of the hot water It should be hot enough that you feel a reaction to sticking in your finger, but not hot enough that you want to pull your finger out.
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon. Some people use a wooden spoon.
  6. Pour
  7. Stop
  8. Knead
  9. Stop
  10. Pour
  11. Cover
  12. If
  13. After
  14. Let
